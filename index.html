How It Works

From audit to execution in 4 steps

01

Audit

Ranktiv crawls your site, analyzes every page against 50+ SEO and AEO criteria, and identifies what's broken, missing, or underperforming.

02

Generate

AI agents generate targeted fixes: meta descriptions, schema markup, FAQ sections, content drafts, refresh plans, competitive briefs, and link opportunities.

03

Approve

Every proposed change appears in your approval queue with a diff preview, expected score impact, and plain-English explanation. Review, approve, edit, or dismiss — nothing deploys without your explicit yes.

04

Execute

Approved changes deploy to your live site automatically. Weekly reports show what changed, what improved, and what to focus on next. One-click revert if anything looks off. Agents learn from your approvals over time.
