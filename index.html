Technical Audit Engine

Crawls all published pages, identifies missing schema, broken internal links, orphan pages, thin content, and AEO gaps. Generates prioritized fix list, then queues the fixes for approval.

How It Works

From audit to execution in 4 steps

01

Audit

Ranktiv crawls your site, analyzes every page against 50+ SEO and AEO criteria, and identifies what's broken, missing, or underperforming.

02

Generate

AI agents generate targeted fixes: meta descriptions, schema markup, FAQ sections, internal link suggestions, entity annotations, and structural improvements.

03

Approve

Every proposed change appears in your approval queue with a diff preview and plain-English explanation. Review, approve, or dismiss — nothing deploys without your explicit yes.

04

Execute

Approved changes deploy to your live site automatically. Weekly reports show what changed, what improved, and what to focus on next. One-click revert if anything looks off.
